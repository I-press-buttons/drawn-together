
COUPLE QUESTION GAME

RARITIES
Common (Easy & Fun)
Uncommon (Interesting Stories)
Rare (Relationship Focused)
Epic (Deeper Reflection)
Legendary (Very Deep)
Mythic (One Per Night)

====================

====================
OPTIONAL GAME RULES
====================
Competitive Scoring:
Common = 1 point
Uncommon = 2 points
Rare = 3 points
Epic = 5 points
Legendary = 10 points
Mythic = 20 points

Cooperative Mode:
Reach 50 total questions answered together.

Dream Jar:
Whenever one of you mentions a future goal, trip, tradition, purchase, or dream, write it down and place it in a Dream Jar. Revisit the jar every few months to see what has been accomplished and what still inspires you.
